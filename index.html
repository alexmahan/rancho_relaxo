Rancho Relaxo

Located in the idyllic mountain town of Rhododendron, Oregon on the edge of the Mt. Hood National Forest and close to world class hiking, mountain biking, rock climbing, winter sports, and nature. Seriously just a short drive from:

  • Sandy Ridge mountain biking trail
  • The Timberline Trail
  • The Pacific Crest Trail
  • Mt. Hood Meadows
  • Ski Bowl
  • French's Dome
  • ...and many many other great spots!
